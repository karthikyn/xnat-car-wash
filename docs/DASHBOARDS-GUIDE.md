# Analytics Dashboards Guide

## Overview
Comprehensive analytics system for xNAT Car Wash Services with 6 specialized dashboards covering all aspects of business operations across 10 service centers.

## Access
- **URL**: `dashboards.html`
- **Authentication**: Admin users only
- **Link**: Available from main admin dashboard

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. 📊 Overview Dashboard
**Purpose**: High-level business metrics and performance summary

**Key Metrics**:
- Total bookings count
- Total revenue (₹87L annually for 10K bookings)
- Gross profit with 35.11% margin
- Average customer rating

**Visualizations**:
- Service distribution chart (Basic 50%, Premium 35%, Deluxe 15%)
- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Service center performance comparison

**Use Cases**:
- Quick business health check
- Executive reporting
- Trend identification

---

### 2. 💬 Customer Feedback Dashboard
**Purpose**: Analyze customer satisfaction and sentiment

**Key Metrics**:
- Total feedback count

- Response rate tracking

**Features**:
- Rating distribution (1-5 stars)
- Sentiment analysis (Positive/Neutral/Negative)
- Recent feedback list with details

**Insights**:

## Key Improvement Areas

### 1. Increase Premium Mix
- Current: 35% Premium + 15% Deluxe = 50%
- Target: 60% high-margin services
- Impact: +₹5-7L annual profit

### 2. Optimize Underperforming Centers
- Identify centers below 70% efficiency
- Implement targeted improvements
- Potential: +15-20% revenue per center

### 3. Boost Customer Retention
- Focus on converting one-time to regular
- Implement loyalty programs
- Target: 85% retention rate

### 4. Dynamic Pricing
- Peak hour pricing (10 AM - 2 PM)
- Weekend premiums
- Potential: +10-15% revenue

### 5. Reduce Operating Costs
- Current: 83.8% of gross profit
- Target: 75% of gross profit
- Impact: +₹2-3L net profit
